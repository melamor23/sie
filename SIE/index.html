Sie Energy seamlessly merges experience and creativity to foster the competitive generation of energy and energy carriers from green field and mature plays, through schemes that have a strong foundation on innovation and sustainability.

Passion for Knowledge and Technology

Our comprehensive team of founders and professionals strive for the use of existing knowledge and best practices, along with both proven and innovative technologies to enable to production of energy and energy carriers from plays where others have missed hidden opportunities, or have lacked the know-how to develop assets in an efficient way.



  • Knowledge Integration
    Our professionals specialize in integrating knowledge across disciplines, from the subsurface, through production and processing, to product commercialization, thanks to individuals with academic degrees in multiple science and engineering fields, and with wide managerial exposure.
  • Vast Experience
    Our company benefits from the vast experience and industrial exposure of partners and senior talent, who have worked on super-majors, independents, national energy companies, and renowned service providers, in different countries and social and environmental settings.
  • Regional Focus
    From our offices in Houston and Colombia, we pursue business opportunities in the Americas, with special focus in USA, Colombia, Peru and Ecuador, where we see plenty of room for us to complement the current energy resources production and commercialization capabilities.


Our Vision for a Lower Carbon World

From our strong core in the hydrocarbons industry, we aspire to gradually transfer skills and capabilities to the extraction of geothermal energy, and the storage of carbon dioxide in the subsurface.
